## Calculations

### Movement per unit time

**Every second:** Move second hand by 6 degrees
**Every 60 seconds:** Move minute hand by 6 degrees
**Every 360 seconds:** Move hour hand by 30 degrees

### Starting Angle Formulas

**Hour hand:** 0.5 * (60 * H + M)
**Minute hand:** 6 * M
**Second hand:** 6 * S
